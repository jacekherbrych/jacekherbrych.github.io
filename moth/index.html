MultiOrbital systems: Training and Horizons (MOTH) is a Doctoral Network within Horizon Europe Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (Horizon-MSCA-DN 2025 project under Grant Agreement No. X). It consolidates seven academic institutions

  • Wrocław University of Science and Technology, Poland (WUST)
  • Ecole Superieure de Physique et Chimie Industrielles de la Ville de Paris, France (ESPCI)
  • Jožef Stefan Institute, Slovenia (JSI)
  • University of Geneva, Switzerland (UG)
  • University of Warsaw, Poland (UW)
  • University of Stuttgart, Germany (US)
  • University of Zagreb, Croatia (UZ)
and a industry partner
  • Quantumz.io, Poland (QMZ)
